Fitness and Exercise Guide

Regular physical activity improves strength, flexibility, and overall health.

Warm-up:
Always start with a warm-up to avoid injury. Examples: jumping jacks, stretching.

Strength Training:
Builds muscles and improves metabolism. Examples: squats, push-ups, lunges.

Cardio Exercises:
Improves heart health. Examples: running, cycling, skipping.

Common Exercises:

Push-ups:
Work on chest, shoulders, and arms.

Squats:
Strengthen legs and core.

Plank:
Improves core strength and stability.

Lunges:
Improve balance and leg strength.

Bicep Curls:
Build arm muscles.

Workout Tips:
Maintain proper form to avoid injury.
Start with low intensity and gradually increase.
Take rest between sets.

Daily Routine:
Exercise at least 30 minutes per day.
Combine cardio and strength training.

Beginner Tips:
Start with bodyweight exercises.
Focus on consistency rather than intensity.

Fitness Guide

For muscle building:
Do strength training like push-ups, squats, lunges.

For beginners:
Start with light workouts and increase gradually.

Warm-up:
Stretching and jumping jacks before workout.

Cool-down:
Stretch muscles after exercise.

Maintain correct posture to avoid injury.
