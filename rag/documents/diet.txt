Diet and Nutrition Guide

A balanced diet is essential for maintaining good health and improving fitness performance. It includes the right proportion of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Proteins:
Proteins help in muscle growth and repair. Sources include eggs, chicken, fish, beans, and lentils.

Carbohydrates:
Carbohydrates provide energy. Good sources are rice, oats, fruits, and vegetables.

Fats:
Healthy fats support hormone production. Sources include nuts, seeds, olive oil, and avocados.

Hydration:
Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Hydration improves metabolism and prevents fatigue.

Pre-Workout Meal:
Eat a light meal 1-2 hours before exercise. Include carbs and protein.

Post-Workout Meal:
Consume protein and carbs after workout to recover muscles.

Weight Loss Diet:
Eat fewer calories, avoid junk food, increase protein and fiber intake.

Muscle Gain Diet:
Increase calorie intake and consume more protein.

Avoid:
Processed foods, excess sugar, and soft drinks.

Pre-Workout Meal:

Before a workout, you should eat a light meal that includes carbohydrates and protein.

Carbohydrates provide energy for your workout. Examples include fruits, oats, and rice.

Protein helps in muscle repair and growth. Examples include eggs, milk, nuts, and chicken.

Avoid heavy meals before workout as they can cause discomfort.

Drink water before starting exercise to stay hydrated.

Healthy Fats Guide

Healthy fats are important for body function.

Good sources:
- Nuts (almonds, walnuts)
- Seeds (chia, flax)
- Avocado
- Olive oil
- Fish (salmon)

Avoid:
- Trans fats
- Fried foods

Healthy fats support brain and heart health.

What is a balanced diet?:
A balanced diet includes protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals.

What should I eat before workout?:
Eat light carbohydrates like banana, oats, or toast 30-60 minutes before workout.

What should I eat after workout?:
Consume protein-rich foods like eggs, chicken, paneer, or protein shake.

How much water should I drink daily?:
Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily.

What foods help in muscle gain?:
Protein-rich foods like chicken, fish, eggs, lentils, and milk help build muscle.

What foods should I avoid for weight loss?:
Avoid junk food, sugary drinks, and processed foods.

What is a high-protein diet?:
A high-protein diet includes foods like eggs, meat, fish, beans, and dairy.

Is eating carbs bad?:
No, carbohydrates provide energy. Choose healthy carbs like rice, oats, and fruits.

What is a healthy breakfast?:
Oats, fruits, eggs, and milk make a healthy breakfast.

Can I eat before sleep?:
Light meals like fruits or milk are okay before sleep.

How many calories should I eat daily?:
It depends on your age, gender, and activity level.

What is calorie deficit?:
Eating fewer calories than you burn helps in weight loss.

Is fast food unhealthy?:
Yes, it contains high fat, sugar, and salt.

How to gain weight healthily?:
Eat more protein, healthy fats, and calories.

Are supplements necessary?:
No, natural food is usually enough for most people.

What are healthy snacks?:

What is intermittent fasting?:
It is an eating pattern with fasting and eating periods.

What is BMI?:
