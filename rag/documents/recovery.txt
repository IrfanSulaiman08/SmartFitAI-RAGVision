Recovery and Rest Guide

Recovery is essential for muscle growth and injury prevention.

Importance of Rest:
Muscles grow during rest, not during workout.

Sleep:
Get 7-9 hours of sleep daily for proper recovery.

Stretching:
Stretch after workout to improve flexibility and reduce soreness.

Hydration:
Drink enough water to help muscle recovery.

Nutrition:
Consume protein after workout for muscle repair.

Active Recovery:
Light activities like walking or yoga help recovery.

Avoid Overtraining:
Too much exercise can cause injuries and fatigue.

Signs of Overtraining:
Fatigue, muscle pain, lack of motivation.

Recovery Techniques:
Foam rolling, massage, cold showers.

Mental Recovery:
Relax your mind through meditation or breathing exercises.
